1. Future Forecasting of Covid-19 Using ML Model

It demonstrates the ML models to forecast the number of upcoming patients will be affected by COVID-19.

  • Implemented machine learning models (RF, LR, SVM, ES) for predicting the future COVID-19 cases.
  • Analyzed and presented forecasts for newly infected cases, deaths, and recoveries.
  • Noteworthy performance observed in the Exponential Smoothing (ES) model, particularly in identifying the number of cases.
  • Technology Used: Python, ML, CSS and HTML.

2. Drinking Water Availability and Facilities

Analyze the accurate water quality classification by considering some factors of water.

  • Implemented a binary classifier using K-Nearest Neighbours and Decision Tree Classifiers to assess the potability of drinking water.
  • Utilized distribution graphs, scatter plots, and heat maps for comprehensive visualization and understanding of the dataset.
  • Implemented K-Nearest Neighbours and Decision Tree Classifiers and Achieved an impressive accuracy rate of 93%.
  • The independent variables like pH, Hardness, Chloramines, Sulfates, Phosphates, Turbidity, Conductivity along with target variable Potability to know the purity of Water.
